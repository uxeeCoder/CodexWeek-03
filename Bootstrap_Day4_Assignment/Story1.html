The Most Beautiful Princess

Once upon a time there was the most beautiful princess ever, who lived in a castle far far away. She was very lonely and wanted to find a prince to marry. She had a magic mirror that she would talk to every day. She would ask the mirror, "Mirror, mirror on the wall, who is the fairest of them all?" The mirror would always reply, "You are the fairest of them all, my princess." The princess was happy with this answer, but she wanted to find a prince to marry. One day, the princess was walking in the forest and she saw a handsome prince riding on a white horse. She fell in love with him instantly and asked him to marry her. The prince said yes and they lived happily ever after.
